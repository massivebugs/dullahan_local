About

Dullahan is a Home Internet-of-Things Network interface.

It enables anyone to integrate their DIY IoT devices with a Raspberry Pi server, and gain access to any device on the home network.

You can also integrate Dullahan with a centralized public server so that you can access and control your devices where ever you are, as long as you are connected to the internet.
